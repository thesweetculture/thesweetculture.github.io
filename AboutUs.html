About Us

The Sweet Culture is a small online shop based in Medan, Indonesia, that specializes in creating unique and delicious snack bouquets. Our mission is to provide a one-of-a-kind snacking experience that combines the sweetness of Indonesian culture with the convenience of online shopping. Our team is made up of individuals who share a passion for creating beautiful and tasty bouquets that make any occasion special. We carefully select only the highest quality snacks, chocolates, and candies to include in our bouquets, ensuring that each bite is as delicious as it is visually appealing. At The Sweet Culture, we believe that snacking should be an enjoyable experience. That's why we strive to create bouquets that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also reflect the sweet culture of Indonesia. We also pride ourselves on our excellent customer service. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that every customer is satisfied with their purchase, from the moment they place their order to the moment their bouquet arrives at their doorstep. Whether you're looking for a gift for a loved one or a treat for yourself, we invite you to indulge in the sweetness of The Sweet Culture.

Meet The Sweet Culture's Founder!

Meet Shabrina Ghasani, the founder of The Sweet Culture. Shabrina is a university culinary student who started the shop as a hobby. Her passion for creating beautiful and tasty snack bouquets for her friends and family inspired her to turn her hobby into a business. As a culinary student, she has a deep understanding of flavor combinations and presentation, which she applies to every bouquet she creates. Her dedication to using only the highest quality ingredients and her attention to detail have made The Sweet Culture a success. She is committed to continuing to create unique and delicious snack bouquets that reflect the sweetness of Indonesian culture.
